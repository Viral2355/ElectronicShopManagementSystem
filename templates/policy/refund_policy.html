Effective Date: January 2026

1. Order Cancellation

Orders can be cancelled before dispatch. Once the product is shipped, cancellation may not be possible due to logistics processing.

2. Refund Eligibility

  • Damaged or defective electronic items upon delivery
  • Incorrect product received
  • Order cancelled within the allowed time window

3. Non-Refundable Cases

Products that are physically damaged after delivery, misused, altered, or missing original packaging may not qualify for refunds.

4. Refund Processing Timeline

Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days and credited to the original payment method.

5. Payment Method Guidelines

For Cash on Delivery (COD) orders, customers may be required to share bank details for manual refund processing.

💡 Our goal is to ensure every refund is handled fairly, securely and as quickly as possible.
